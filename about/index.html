About Gamgee

This is only the beginning.

Beyond AI, we believe that AI meets biology is the biggest opportunity of the 21st century. We're the software company designing custom mRNA cancer vaccines for dogs — and dogs are just where we're starting.

What we do

We design personalised mRNA vaccines, built from your dog's own tumour, engineered to help their immune system recognise it and fight back. We manage the process end to end: genomic sequencing, vaccine design, and treatment under veterinary oncologist supervision.

It started with one dog. Read Rosie's story →

Where we are today

We're not just developing a cancer vaccine for dogs — we're proving it works. Together with our partners, we're currently running clinical trials in Australia, and accepting applications globally.

Current status

In clinical trials now, with treatment already underway.

First treatment began in December 2025. This phase is expected to close around December 2026.
