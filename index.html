The Process

From application to treatment.

If selected, GAMGEE manages the entire process end to end.

01

Review & intake

We review your application and reach out to discuss your dog's case. If there's a fit, we move forward together.

02

Vet outreach & collection

We work with your vet to confirm the diagnosis, collect a tissue sample, and send it to our genomics lab.

03

Vaccine creation

We analyse the data, identify the mutations driving the cancer, and design a personalised protocol.

04

Treatment

Your dog receives their personalised treatment from a veterinary oncologist. We monitor response.
